Top 5 Recipes to Use Leftovers

Leftover Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (chilled)
  • 1 cup mixed leftover vegetables
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Method:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and stir for 30 seconds.
  2. Add leftover vegetables. Sauté for 2–3 minutes until heated.
  3. Push veggies to the side. Crack in the egg and scramble (optional).
  4. Add chilled rice, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Mix well and cook for 3–5 mins.
  5. Serve hot and garnish with green onions or sesame seeds if available.

Stale Bread Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups stale bread cubes
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup raisins (optional)

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease a small baking dish.
  2. Whisk eggs, sugar, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon in a bowl.
  3. Add bread cubes and let them soak for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in raisins if using, pour into the baking dish, and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  5. Cool slightly and serve warm or chilled with cream or ice cream.

Leftover Pasta Bake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked pasta
  • 1 cup leftover meat or veggies
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup milk or cream
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Mix pasta, sauce, milk, and leftovers in a bowl.
  2. Pour into a baking dish, top with shredded cheese.
  3. Bake for 20–25 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  4. Let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Leftover Veggie Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chopped leftover veggies
  • 4 cups broth (any type)
  • 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper & herbs

Method:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot. Sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add chopped veggies and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in broth, bring to a boil, then simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add cooked rice, season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  5. Serve warm with toast or crackers.

Leftover Chicken Wraps

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shredded leftover chicken
  • 2 tortillas or flatbreads
  • 1/4 cup chopped veggies (lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber)
  • 2 tbsp yogurt or mayo
  • Salt & pepper

Method:

  1. Mix shredded chicken with yogurt or mayo. Add salt & pepper.
  2. Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  3. Place chicken mix and chopped veggies on each tortilla.
  4. Roll up tightly and slice in half. Serve with sauce or fresh salad.
