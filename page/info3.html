DIY Composting at Home

Composting at home is a simple and eco-friendly way to reduce food waste and enrich your soil. All you need are organic kitchen scraps, a bin, and a little patience. Below are 7 easy-to-compost items that you can start with today. Try it in your backyard or even on a balcony using a small container. It's sustainable and surprisingly fun!

Grass Clippings
Vegetable Scraps
Eggshells
Coffee Grounds
Tea Bags
Fruit Peels
Dry Leaves
Nut Shells
Corn Cobs and Husks
Old Flowers/ Dead Plants
